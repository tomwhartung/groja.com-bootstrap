Books

These books were instrumental in conceiving and implementing the Groja © program.

Jung, Carl. (1921) Psychological Types.
For more information, see the sidebar.
Myers, Isabel Briggs and Myers, Peter. (1980) Gifts Differing.
The name sake behind the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI) Isabel Briggs Myers' work makes Jung's theories easier to understand and even more practical.
Keirsey, David. (1984) Please Understand Me.
This book is nice because it has a very nice quiz that can be taken fairly quickly.
Keirsey, David. (1998) Please Understand Me II.
An updated edition that contains the same quiz but delves more deeply into the descriptions of how people of different types interact.

You can find a copy of any of these at amazon.com.
