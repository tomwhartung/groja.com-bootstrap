Welcome to the Spiritual Portrait Site

The abstract images on this site are called GROJAS or Spiritual Portraits, and each one represents a unique personality.

GROJAs are Graphical Representations of Jungian Archetypes.

Inspired by the work of Piet Mondrian, Carl Jung, Wassily Kandinsky, Isabel Myers-Briggs, and David Keirsey, the Groja Program © creates portraits of individuals based on their score on the MBTI ® or a similar personality questionnaire.

To learn more, explore this site and the galleries at seeourminds.com.

If you're ready to see your personality, or that of a friend or loved one, click to learn how you can get your own personalized GROJA!
