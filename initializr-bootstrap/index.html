Welcome to the Spiritual Portrait Site

Groja: Graphical Representations of Jungian Archetypes.

I am inspired by Myers-Briggs' and Keirsey's work, and cannot praise them enough! But I have my own quiz, ideas and perceptions, and ultimately this is my own thing. All of this is based on Carl Jung's theories so naturally similarities abound.

When I first learned that I was an INFP, it was life-altering. I would like to help you on a similar journey of greater self-awareness!
